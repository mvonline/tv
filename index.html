How to use MasTV

  • TV Remote: Fully optimized for Smart TV remotes and DPads.
  • Navigation: Use Arrows to move, Enter to play.
  • Back/Exit: Press Esc or Backspace to close the player.
  • Direct Channel: Type numbers on your keyboard (e.g. 12 Enter) to instantly jump to a channel.
  • Favourites: Click the ♥ icon to pin channels to the top.
  • Background Play: Use the 📺 button on the player for Picture-in-Picture.
  • Deep Links: Bookmark a channel explicitly via URL (e.g. #bbc).
